The world of JavaScript frameworks is vast and ever-evolving. Choosing the right framework can significantly impact project timelines, performance, and scalability. This article provides a comparative overview of some of the most popular JavaScript frameworks today, including React, Vue, and Angular.

We will delve into their core philosophies, architectural patterns, performance characteristics, ecosystem, and community support. Understanding these aspects will help developers and teams make informed decisions when selecting a framework for their next project.
